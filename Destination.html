Overview

Paris, the capital city of France, is known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its picturesque streets and iconic landmarks attract millions of visitors every year.

History

The history of Paris spans over two millennia. From Roman times to the modern era, the city has played a pivotal role in art, science, and politics.

Culture

Paris is famous for its museums, theaters, and vibrant cultural scene. Festivals, exhibitions, and cafes contribute to its lively atmosphere.

Gallery

Explore the beautiful landmarks: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and charming streets of Montmartre.
