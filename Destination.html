Overview

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, food, and culture. It’s one of the most visited cities in the world.

History

Paris has a rich history spanning over 2,000 years — from Roman times to the modern era, influencing art, politics, and science.

Culture

Paris is famous for its museums, theaters, and lively streets filled with music, festivals, and coffee culture.

Gallery

Famous landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.
